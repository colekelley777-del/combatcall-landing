FAQ

Common Questions

What is CombatCall?
A data-driven analytics tool for UFC and MMA fight cards. We aggregate fighter stats, historical performance, and live odds across major sportsbooks so you can spot value before the line moves.
Is CombatCall a sportsbook?
No. We don’t accept or facilitate wagers. We provide analytics so you can make smarter picks at whichever licensed sportsbook you use.
How accurate are your predictions?
Our models are trained on years of historical fight data and updated as new fights happen. No model is right every time — sports outcomes are inherently uncertain — but our edge cases consistently identify mispriced lines before the market corrects.
Can I try it before paying?
Yes. Sign up with just your email and get the opening prelims of every card free, with full analytics on those three fights. Subscribe to unlock the rest of the card.
How do I cancel?
Sign in to your account, click “Manage Subscription,” and Stripe handles the rest. No phone calls, no retention pop-ups.
Is this available everywhere?
We’re available across the U.S. except Washington State (regulatory).
